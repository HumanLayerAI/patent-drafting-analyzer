## What It Does

A live, interactive Claude artifact that analyzes patent claim language and identifies potential vulnerabilities in IP protection. The system examines claim scope, dependent claim structure, means-plus-function limitations, and prior art exposure risks.

Rather than providing generic patent guidance, the artifact applies structured legal analysis frameworks to help inventors and practitioners examine their claims with prosecution-aware precision.
